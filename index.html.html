Talent Acquisition

The Hiring Process

A step-by-step guide to finding, evaluating, and onboarding the right candidate.

📢
Job Advertisement
Step 01
  • ✏️ Write a clear job title and concise role summary that reflects the actual day-to-day responsibilities.
  • 📋 List required qualifications, skills, and experience so candidates can self-screen effectively.
  • 🌐 Post the role across job boards, LinkedIn, and your company's careers page for maximum reach.
  • 💰 Include salary range and benefits to attract serious applicants and build trust from the start.
🔍
Resume Screening
Step 02
  • 📄 Review applications against must-have criteria to create a focused shortlist quickly.
  • 🏷️ Look for relevant keywords, measurable achievements, and career progression patterns.
  • ⚖️ Use a consistent scoring rubric to keep evaluation objective and free from bias.
  • 📬 Send timely acknowledgements to all applicants, including those not moving forward.
🎙️
Interviews
Step 03
  • 📞 Begin with a brief phone screen to confirm availability, expectations, and communication style.
  • 🧠 Use structured behavioural questions (STAR format) to assess past experience consistently.
  • 🤝 Include a panel interview so multiple team members can evaluate cultural and role fit.
  • ❓ Allow candidates time to ask questions — their curiosity reveals motivation and preparation.
🧪
Assessment Tests
Step 04
  • 💻 Assign a practical task or case study that mirrors real work the candidate would handle.
  • 🧩 Use aptitude or psychometric tests to assess problem-solving and cognitive ability objectively.
  • ⏱️ Keep assessments reasonable in length — respect the candidate's time to maintain goodwill.
  • 📊 Evaluate submissions with a predefined rubric shared across all reviewers for consistency.
🏆
Final Selection
Step 05
  • 🗂️ Compile interview scores, assessment results, and reference checks into a single summary.
  • 👥 Hold a debrief with the hiring team to discuss top candidates and surface any concerns.
  • 📞 Conduct reference checks to validate experience and gain third-party perspective on the candidate.
  • ✅ Make the final decision based on data, team input, and alignment with role requirements.
🎉
Job Offer
Step 06
  • 📩 Extend a verbal offer first to gauge interest and discuss any questions before formalising.
  • 📝 Send a written offer letter with salary, start date, benefits, and key terms clearly outlined.
  • 🔄 Be open to negotiation on compensation or start date within approved boundaries.
  • 🚀 Once accepted, share onboarding details promptly to keep the candidate engaged before day one.
